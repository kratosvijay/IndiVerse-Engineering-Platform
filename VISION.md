# IndiVerse Developer Platform (IDP) - Vision

## Long-Term Direction
The IndiVerse Developer Platform (IDP) is an AI-native engineering platform designed to help developers plan, build, review, test, and maintain software through a provider-agnostic, plugin-first architecture.

Our long-term target is to transition from a framework, to a developer platform, then to an engineering platform, and finally into a fully autonomous Engineering Operating System.

```text
Internal Tool (Framework)
      │
      ▼
Developer Platform
      │
      ▼
Engineering Platform
      │
      ▼
Engineering Operating System
```
